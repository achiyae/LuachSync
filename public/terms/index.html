LuachSync is a client-side web application designed to help users manage Hebrew calendar events (such as birthdays, anniversaries, or yahrzeits) and export or sync them with external calendars, including Google Calendar. LuachSync offers an optional feature to automatically sync your generated events with your Google Calendar.
